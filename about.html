Who We Are

Sunset Safaris is a tourism company based in Ntinda, Kampala, Uganda. We specialize in offering unforgettable safari experiences that allow visitors to explore Uganda’s rich wildlife and beautiful landscapes, especially during the magical sunset hours.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide safe, affordable, and memorable travel experiences while promoting Uganda’s natural beauty and supporting sustainable tourism.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading safari companies in East Africa, known for excellent service and unique travel experiences.
