About Us

Based in Ntinda, Kampala, we offer unforgettable sunset safari experiences that showcase the beauty of Uganda’s wildlife. As the sun sets and the skies glow with warm golden colors, our guided safaris give you the perfect opportunity to see animals becoming more active in their natural habitat. We are committed to providing safe, comfortable, and memorable adventures that allow you to enjoy the peaceful atmosphere and stunning landscapes Uganda has to offer.
